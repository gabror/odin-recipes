Chiken Parmesian

Ingredients

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 cups Italian-seasoned bread crumbs
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 1 jar of marinara sauce
  • 1 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven to 350Â°F.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper.
  3. In one bowl, place the flour. In another bowl, place the beaten eggs. In a third bowl, combine the bread crumbs and Parmesan cheese.
  4. Dredge each chicken breast in the flour, shaking off any excess. Dip the chicken into the beaten eggs, then coat with the bread crumb mixture.
  5. Heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat. Add the chicken and cook for 3-4 minutes per side, or until golden brown.
  6. Transfer the chicken to a baking dish and pour marinara sauce over each chicken breast, then sprinkle shredded mozzarella cheese on top.
  7. Bake for 20-25 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through and the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  8. Serve over pasta or with a side of garlic bread. Enjoy!
