Spicy Sausage and Vegetable Stir Fry

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound spicy sausage, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 yellow onion, sliced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 2 cups cooked rice (optional)
  1. In a large pan or wok, heat the olive oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the sliced sausage and cook for about 3-4 minutes, until browned.
  3. Add the sliced red pepper, onion, garlic, mushrooms, and carrots to the pan. Stir fry for an additional 5-7 minutes, until the vegetables are tender.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Stir in the soy sauce and red pepper flakes (if using).
  6. Serve over cooked rice if desired.

Enjoy your Spicy Sausage and Vegetable Stir Fry!
